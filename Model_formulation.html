Multi-stage Transportation Scheduling Flow Optimization

Prompt

A manufactoring company make network routers has projected demand for each time periods is known. Because of the non-zero transportation lead-time, it can meet the demand by either producing some amount of routers prior to and/or by drawing upon the inventorying carried from the previous period. Unmet demand will be pushed to the next time period.

Diagram

Assumptions

  • Demand info known for the entire planning horizon
  • Shipment arrangement made at the begining of the planning horizon
  • CM with unlimited and instant supply

Cost (Per unit):

  • Transportation cost from contracted manufacturors to Direct Fulfillment warehoue . In this scenario, multiple transportation modes are considered, with faster mode associated with higher transporation cost.
  • Inventory carry cost whenever an unit of product at is rolled over to
  • Penalty cost whenever there is an unmet demand roll over from to

Links - decision variables

  • One link connecting from to to denote the roll over demand at DF, the cost of this cost will be inventory holding cost
  • One link connecting from to to denote demand fulfillment, the cost of this link is zero
  • One link connecting from to to denote unmet demand, the cost of this link will be customer waiting cost

Constraint

  • Inventory level at DF doesn't exceed maximum inventory level at all time
  • Maximum transportation capacity for a specific mode of transportation
  • Flow convervation at each node
